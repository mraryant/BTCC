Frequently Asked questions

Welcome to our FAQ section, designed to address common queries and provide helpful information about our services.

Q: How does BTCC contribute to sustainability?

BTCC adopts a proof-of-stake (PoS) consensus, significantly reducing energy consumption compared to traditional proof-of-work mechanisms and contributing to a more sustainable blockchain ecosystem.

Q: How can I participate in BTCC governance?

Holders of BTCC have the opportunity to participate in governance decisions, including voting on protocol upgrades and proposals, ensuring an inclusive and democratic governance structure.

Q: What makes BTCC scalable?

BTCC incorporates sharding technology and off-chain transactions, enhancing scalability by processing a higher volume of transactions concurrently, ensuring a smooth user experience.

Q: How user-friendly is the BTCC platform?

BTCC prioritizes a user-centric design, offering an intuitive interface that makes navigating decentralized finance as easy as using traditional financial services.
